Atlanta Workspace Directory

This is a list of workspaces found while looking for office space. Help me fill in the blank spots. I hope it helps.

Criteria for Inclusion

  • This is not craigslist, only permanent shared and coworking spaces are included.
  • Locations should have a significant focus on technology companies.

Corrections or Additions?

  • If you are on GitHub, you can send me a Pull Request.
  • Or, just shoot me an email at cameronc@gmail.com.
  • You can see the changelog of history.

Other Curated Directories

  • Check out Atlanta's page on The Coworking Wiki.
